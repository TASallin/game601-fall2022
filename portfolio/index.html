(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Escape House
Escape House is a semi-cooperative card game where 6-8 players can try and solve obstacles cooperatively or selfishly. It is themed around escaping from a cult, and if players take too many rounds to solve all the puzzles, the cult will return, and everyone will lose. If all the puzzles are solved, then the players win, except for any who were betrayed in the process.
PLAY
